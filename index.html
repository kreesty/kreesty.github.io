About

Hey there, I'm Cristian, a dynamic indie game developer and software engineer with a passion for technology and digital entertainment. Driven by a desire to innovate and collaborate on exciting new projects, I bring over 8 years of experience in the gaming industry to the table.

Specializing in C# programming within the Unity engine, I have collaborated with talented artists to craft immersive gameplay experiences that captivate players. Beyond Unity, I've broadened my skill set by working with other game engines like Unreal Engine and GameMaker, enhancing my versatility in game development.

My expertise extends beyond programming; I excel in designing and implementing game mechanics, user interfaces, and AI systems, creating seamless and engaging player experiences. Additionally, I possess a working knowledge of 3D modeling with Blender, allowing me to create environmental props to enrich game worlds.

Feel free to explore my portfolio showcasing my games and tools. If you're interested in collaborating or discussing potential projects, don't hesitate to reach out. I am always eager to explore new opportunities and work alongside fellow professionals to bring fresh ideas to life.
